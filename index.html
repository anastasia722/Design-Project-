About me and this article:

My name is Anastasia Papapostolou. I am 14 years old and go to St. Thomas high school. I enjoy watching football with my dad, playing games with my family, hanging out with my friends, reading and listening to music. I am usually searching for a good tv show to watch. Because of that, I decided to make a website giving teens like me suggestions of shows to watch. In this website you will find trailers, reviews, summaries and much more.
